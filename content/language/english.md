## Achieve British Accent

1. Choice of words
   1. Trash - USA
   2. Rubbish - British
2. Sound `/tu:/`
   1. Americans pronounce `/t` $\implies$ `/d`
   2. pronounce t between the vowels
   3. city - `/'sIt.i/`
   4. [water](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X6_8p3R-ZBI)
   5. whatever - glottal t
   6. [daughter](https://youtu.be/LAb7l9OWuR8)
   7. proper
