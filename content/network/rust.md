::: tip runtime
Most of the databases are written in languages that have a runtime. It takes away some performance.
:::

* `C++` has very small runtime, but then Memory management becomes your problem.
* [SEGV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Segmentation_fault)
  * Maybe good thing
  * Heartbleed, cloudbleed, WannaCry

> Speed and safety guarentees by Rust

* Check at compiletime, **Ownership**
* Data has one owner
* Data is borrowed, immutable by default
  * Could be made mutable
