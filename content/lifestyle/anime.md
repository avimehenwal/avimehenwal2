## How to make one

> 30 mins episode can have **15,000** pictures

1. script
2. storyboard
   1. drawing
   2. movements
   3. panning of camera
   4. length of each shot
3. layouts of scenery
   1. landscape department
   2. handmade
      1. dragon ball z
   3. digital backgrounds
      1. one punch man
4. Key animation
   1. first frame
   2. middle frame
   3. end frame
5. In between animators
6. compositing
   1. coloring
   2. shading
7. VFX
   1. ligtning glows
   2. auras
   3. blurrs
   4. glints on swords
   5. smoke
   6. fire
8. Post production
9. Voice overs
